mission "get heliarch"
	landing
	name "New equipment"
	description "Head to <destination> to check out the shipyards and outfitter you've paid to get access to."
	source
		government "Coalition"
		not planet "Far Home"
	destination "Far Home"
	to offer
		has "joined the lunarium"
		not "joined the heliarchs"
		"credits" > 5000000
	on offer
		conversation
			scene "scene/kimek_spaceport"
			`As you enter <origin>'s spaceport, a suspiciously nervous Kimek approaches. "Hello Captain <last>. About a not entirely legal business opportunity, to you I would like to speak. Inside your ship, can we perhaps talk further?"`
			choice
				`	Sure.`
					goto yes
				`	I'm not interested.`
					decline
			label yes
			scene "scene/kimek_sitting"
			`Once aboard your ship, the Kimek begins to relax. "Already built a few shipyards and outfitters in the 'Far Home' system my client has, unlicensed ships and equipment, some with. Now looking for paying customers, my client is. For a small fee of five million credits, granted access to these difficult-to-access technologies, you will be. Heliarch and Lunarium technologies, to be precise. Require payment immediately, I will, and provided with a Heliarch license also will you be.. Are you interested?"`
			choice
				`	That sounds great, I will pay.`
					goto yesyes
				`	No, I don't feel comfortable with that.`
					decline
			label yesyes
			scene "scene/kimek_sitting"
			`"Very good." the Kimek replies and pulls a golden Heliarch circlet out of his pocket. He places it on your head and connects it to a small electronic box with a cable. After about 20 seconds the whole procedure is over and the Heliarch license is activated. "And now please transfer the credits." He holds out a pad with bank details, which you confirm. After everything has been arranged, the Kimek leaves your ship. You should head to <destination> to take a look at the new equipment available.`
				accept
	on accept
		payment -5000000
		event "both sides"
		set "license: Heliarch"
	on complete
		conversation
			`You've reached <planet>. The local outfitters and shipyards should have all the equipment the Coalition, the Heliarchs and the Lunarium can offer. `
